template<class... ArgN>
auto rxcpp::blocking_observable< T, Observable >::subscribe ( ArgN &&...  an) const -> composite_subscription
inline

subscribe will cause this observable to emit values to the provided subscriber. callers must provide enough arguments to make a subscriber. overrides are supported. thus subscribe(thesubscriber, composite_subscription()) will take thesubscriber.get_observer() and the provided subscription and subscribe to the new subscriber. the on_next, on_error, on_completed methods can be supplied instead of an observer if a subscription or subscriber is not provided then a new subscription will be created.
